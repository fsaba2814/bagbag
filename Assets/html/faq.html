Shipping & Delivery

Yes — we ship worldwide. Delivery times vary by region. Most orders arrive within 7–14 business days internationally, and 3–5 days within Pakistan.

Shipping is FREE on all orders over Rs 5,000 within Pakistan. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package directly on the courier's website.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in their original packaging. Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned.

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. You will be notified via email once approved.

Product & Care

Apply a leather conditioner every 6 months. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight or rain. Store in a dust bag when not in use. Wipe with a dry cloth after use.

All premium leather bags come with a lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects. This does not cover normal wear and tear or damage caused by misuse.
